Bio

I am 20 years old as of 2025 and have a litany of goals I would like to reach before 2030. That year used to seem so far away, but is now just around the corner. I am from a large Utahn family, an active LDS member, and a self-described cinemaphile. I am in love with stories in all sorts of media. I enjoy writing when I’m on my own, playing Dungeons and Dragons with friends, and watching movies with strangers in theaters.

I apply the same story progression recognition skills into the work I do as a Data Analyst. I currently work for AdvisX, an audit firm, specializing in Anti-Money Laundering (“AML”) System Validation. I process large loads of data, assess the quality of investigations, and discover where inaccuracies or inefficiencies may be occurring. To fully understand the quality of the systems, it is required to understand the flow of data, the meaning and source behind every transaction, and apply analytical data to real-life scenarios of money laundering or other crimes.

The blend of my creative passions and my corporate leaning, I believe, is one of my best strengths. It is valuable to have an immense amount of knowledge, it is more valuable to know how to apply it.
